Students learn the fundamentals of data stewardship through a six-week course focused on drafting a data re-use strategy. Sessions guide participants from identifying demand for data to translating data-driven insights into action. All lectures and coursework focus on public sector examples based in Europe, and the course meets for three in-person sessions. The final session is devoted to peer-led presentations.

## Policies

- Must be 18 years or older to enroll
- Must participate in a professional capacity on an individual basis (not as a team)
- Must be willing and able to submit assignments for review
- Must attend all sessions
- Must possess English language proficiency

## Prerequisite

- Applicants must attend all courses in person in Berlin.

Interested participants should complete the application form. For questions, email datastewards@thegovlab.org.
