Data stewardship is evolving rapidly. Explore the key trends that will shape the future of data stewardship in the years to come.

## Youth Perspectives

At the AFS Youth Assembly, young leaders shared their insights on digital well-being and data rights, highlighting the importance of including youth voices in data governance discussions.

## Key Takeaways

- Youth demand greater transparency in how their data is used
- Digital well-being requires holistic approaches to data stewardship
- Intergenerational dialogue is essential for responsible data futures
