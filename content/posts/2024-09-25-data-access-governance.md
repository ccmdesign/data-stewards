The State of Open Data Policy Summit is an annual conference hosted by the Open Data Policy Lab (a collaboration between The GovLab and Microsoft) to explore how policy and technology shape access to data for public interest re-use.

## Summit Overview

This year's summit brought together policymakers, technologists, and data stewards to address critical questions about data access in an AI-driven world.

## Key Themes

- **Data Readiness**: Preparing datasets for responsible AI training
- **Governance Frameworks**: Establishing trust and accountability
- **Policy Innovation**: New approaches to data sharing and reuse
