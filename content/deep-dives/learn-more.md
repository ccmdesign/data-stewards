# Deep Dives Learn More

## Program Snapshot

- **Schedule:** Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday from 3:00–6:00pm CET
- **Location:** Online with facilitation by TDT GovLab
- **Tuition:** €200 per participant
- **Capacity:** Maximum 30 participants (applications close once 25 seats are filled)

## Lecturer

**Stefaan Verhulst** leads the masterclass as Co-Founder and Chief Research & Development Officer at The GovLab. He brings deep expertise in strategic data partnerships and responsible stewardship.

### Masterclass Focus

#### The Data Stewards Canvas

*Stewarding Strategic Data Futures*

This intensive session equips experienced stewards to apply the Data Stewards Canvas as a practical framework for orchestrating technical, legal, and ethical dimensions of data re-use.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the evolving role of data stewards across global data re-use ecosystems
- Practice with the Data Stewards Canvas as a repeatable tool for stewardship engagements
- Identify how stewardship bridges technical, legal, and ethical requirements for trusted data collaborations

## Structure

- **Concept Development (20 minutes):** Mini-lecture on the history and rationale of stewardship
- **Interactive Exercise (20 minutes):** Guided walkthrough of the Data Stewards Canvas
- **Discussion (10 minutes):** Open dialogue to pressure-test real-world applications
- **Summary & Wrap-Up (10 minutes):** Key takeaways, next steps, and resource pathways

## Participant Benefits

- Certificate of Completion
- Access to a 200+ member global alumni community of data stewards
- Advanced understanding of innovative stewardship perspectives and practices
