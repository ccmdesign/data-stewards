Our flagship, hands-on program for leaders seeking to build data stewardship strategies for their organizations. Learn the fundamentals of open data, data science, and data collaboration.

### What You'll Learn

- The core principles of responsible data stewardship
- How to design and govern data initiatives using our 4P Framework (Purpose, Principles, Processes, Practices)
- How to establish a social license for data re-use
- Models for developing data sharing agreements, evaluation KPIs, and trust mechanisms
- A capstone plan to implement in your organization
