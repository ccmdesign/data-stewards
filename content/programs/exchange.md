Our vibrant network for practitioners, alumni, and partners to stay engaged and share knowledge.

### Ways to Engage

- Data Stewardship Slack channel for sharing lessons, tools, and trends with fellow course participants
- Events and salons featuring thought leaders in intimate virtual and in-person formats
- Datathons that bring practitioners together for collaborative sprints on real governance challenges
- Trends to Watch briefings covering emerging topics like AI governance, data sovereignty, and cross-sector collaborations
