# Vue.js Monthly Meetup - November

Join us for our monthly Vue.js community meetup! This is a casual gathering where developers of all skill levels come together to share experiences, learn new techniques, and build connections.

## Agenda

**6:00 PM - 6:30 PM: Networking & Pizza**
Grab a slice and meet fellow Vue enthusiasts. Share what you're working on and make new connections.

**6:30 PM - 7:15 PM: Lightning Talks**
- "Migrating to Nuxt 4" - Sarah Chen
- "Component Testing Strategies" - Mike Rodriguez
- "Building Design Systems with Nuxt UI" - Jessica Park

**7:15 PM - 8:00 PM: Main Presentation**
"Advanced State Management Patterns in Vue 3"
Deep dive into modern state management approaches, including Pinia best practices and composable patterns.

**8:00 PM - 9:00 PM: Open Discussion & Networking**
Bring your questions, challenges, and ideas. This is your chance to get feedback from the community.

## What to Bring

- Your laptop if you want to share code
- Business cards for networking
- Your enthusiasm for Vue!

## Venue

The meetup will be held at TechHub San Francisco, a short walk from the Montgomery Street BART station. The space is wheelchair accessible.

123 Market Street, Suite 400
San Francisco, CA 94105

## RSVP

Space is limited to 50 attendees, so please RSVP early. Pizza and drinks will be provided!
