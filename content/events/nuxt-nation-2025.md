# Nuxt Nation 2025

Get ready for the most anticipated Nuxt conference of the year! Nuxt Nation 2025 brings together developers, designers, and enthusiasts from around the world to celebrate the Nuxt ecosystem.

## What to Expect

### Keynote Presentations
Hear from the Nuxt core team about the latest features, roadmap, and vision for the future of Nuxt.

### Technical Workshops
Hands-on sessions covering:
- Advanced Nuxt patterns and best practices
- Building full-stack applications with Nuxt
- Performance optimization techniques
- Server-side rendering deep dive

### Networking Opportunities
Connect with fellow developers, share experiences, and build lasting relationships in the Nuxt community.

## Schedule Highlights

**Morning Sessions (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)**
- Opening keynote
- State of Nuxt 2025
- Workshop: Building scalable applications

**Afternoon Sessions (1:00 PM - 6:00 PM)**
- Advanced patterns and architectures
- Community showcases
- Panel discussion with core team
- Closing remarks

## Speakers

We're excited to announce an amazing lineup of speakers including:
- Nuxt core team members
- Industry experts from leading tech companies
- Community contributors and advocates

Early bird tickets are now available! Choose between in-person attendance in Amsterdam or join us virtually from anywhere in the world.
